How to freely browse the internet in China

There are many restrictions to using the internet (most of them have to do with media potentially opposing the governmnet and their policies) but they can be dodged, potentially.

Some solutions already exist but there are more that are just ideas that may work.

  • Using a VPN
  • Using a proxy server
  • Disguising Websites to look like they promote "The Party"
  • Using a mirror site (a website that replicates a normal website)
